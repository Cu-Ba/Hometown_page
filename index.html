Enjoy the feeling of the old american shaped towns or lose yourself into the royal spanish buildings.

Dance until the sun rise again, enjoy every beat of the local culture or just play along with the percussion.

From crystal like water to fast flowing rivers, dive, swim, sail. Every plan is as good as the beaches.
